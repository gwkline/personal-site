Led a team of engineers in implementing a distributed data collection and enrichment pipeline, allowing associates to aggregate up-to-date data in minutes instead of days, resulting in 20% fewer bounce-backs.

Engineered custom AI-driven services for intelligent email responses and follow-ups, employing fine-tuned machine learning models to ensure personalized and timely engagement.
